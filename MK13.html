The Mark 13 Missile Launcher is a single-arm, ship-mounted guided missile launcher. It is capable of firing RIM-66 Standard and RIM-24 Tartar missiles for anti-air defense and the RGM-84 Harpoon missile for anti-ship purposes. Below the launcher itself lies a 40 round magazine. Although it has since been removed from the US Navy’s arsenal, it still sees service in several other countries and on old donated US warships.

This model is built upon the rotation base, the arm can elevate up and down, and comes with a RIM-66 Standard missile. Where you might put this build, we have no clue, unless you have a 1:35 scale Oliver Hazard Perry class frigate lying around.
